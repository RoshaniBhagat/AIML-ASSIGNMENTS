Assignment (27/03/2026) 
Assignment Name : Semantic Meaning 
Description : Find 5 word pairs and explain semantic similarity.

Word Pairs with Explanation
1. Happy – Joyful
Both words express positive emotions and happiness
High semantic similarity

2. Big – Large
Both refer to size or magnitude
 Nearly identical meaning

3. Fast – Quick
 Both indicate speed
Used interchangeably in most contexts

4. Smart – Intelligent
Both describe mental ability or intelligence
Very closely related

5. Car – Vehicle
“Car” is a type of “Vehicle”
Semantic relationship (category-based similarity)

Explanation
Semantic similarity refers to how closely related two words are in meaning. Words can be similar because they:
Mean the same (synonyms)
Belong to the same category
Represent related concepts

Conclusion
Understanding semantic similarity helps in Natural Language Processing tasks such as text analysis, search engines, and chatbots, where meaning is more important than exact words.
