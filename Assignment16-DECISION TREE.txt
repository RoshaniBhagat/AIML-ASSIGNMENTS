Assignment Name : Decision Tree on Paper
Description : Draw a decision tree predicting whether you should play outside.


A decision tree helps in making decisions based on conditions. Here we predict whether to play outside or not depending on weather conditions.

                Weather
               /       \
            Sunny     Rainy
             /           \
      Humidity High     Windy?
        /        \       /    \
      No        Yes    Yes    No
    (Don't      Play   Don't  Play
      Play)             Play

Explanation:-
The root node is the weather condition (Sunny or Rainy).
If the weather is Sunny, we check the humidity level:
High humidity → Do not play outside
Normal humidity → Play outside
If the weather is Rainy, we check whether it is windy:
Windy → Do not play outside
Not windy → Play outside

Conclusion:-

The decision tree helps us make a simple decision by evaluating conditions step by step. It is widely used in machine learning for classification problems, where data is split into branches based on rules.
